## OpenAPI specification sources

Canonical, always-current spec URLs (English): `https://console.openapi.com/oas/en/<service>.openapi.json` — the full list is in [`oas/00-list.txt`](oas/00-list.txt). A snapshot of all 29 specs is vendored in [`knowledge/oas/`](oas/), and per-service endpoint summaries generated from them live in [`knowledge/services/`](services/).
